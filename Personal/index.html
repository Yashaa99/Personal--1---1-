Prof. Dr. Mohammed Moshiul Hoque

Academic & Research Journey

Prof. Dr. Mohammed Moshiul Hoque is a leading scholar in AI, Machine Learning, and NLP, and founder of the CUET NLP Lab. He has over 220 publications in journals and conferences, with key works in HRI, speech & image processing, and Bengali language technology. Beyond research, he leads the Sheikh Kamal IT Business Incubator (SKIBI), organizes AI/ML/NLP workshops, and conducts youth ICT skill development training, contributing significantly to innovation and professional development in Bangladesh’s technology sector.
